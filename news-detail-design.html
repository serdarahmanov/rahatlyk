Rahatlyk Wins
Best Beverage
Brand 2025

Published 15 November 2025
Category Company News
Reading time 3 min

The Rahatlyk team at the Central Asia Business Excellence Awards, November 2025.

We are proud to announce that Rahatlyk has been recognised as the Best Beverage Brand at the Central Asia Business Excellence Awards 2025. This prestigious award celebrates our unwavering commitment to delivering pure, natural water to homes and businesses across Turkmenistan.

The award reflects the trust our customers place in every bottle of Rahatlyk water. We remain dedicated to the highest standards of quality, sourced from protected mountain springs and processed with world-class filtration technology.

Rahatlyk's protected mountain spring — the foundation of every bottle.

Every drop of Rahatlyk is a promise — pure, natural, and sourced from the mountains that have nourished this land for centuries.

Rahatlyk · 2025

This recognition comes after a year of significant investment in our production facilities, sustainable sourcing practices, and community engagement across Turkmenistan. We are grateful to the dedicated teams behind every stage of our process — from the spring to your glass.

Our state-of-the-art filtration facility in Ashgabat.

Looking ahead, we remain committed to expanding our reach while preserving the environmental integrity of our water sources. 2026 will see the launch of our zero-waste packaging initiative and new refrigerated distribution hubs across all five regions of Turkmenistan.
